## Games

### Warp Lines
A kid-friendly abstract strategy board game inspired by checkers, played on a unique graph-based board.

**Features:**
- 🎮 Two game modes: Player vs Player and Player vs Computer
- 🤖 **AI with 4 Difficulty Levels:**
  - **Easy**: AI looks 2 moves ahead, 10 pieces per side
  - **Medium**: AI looks 3 moves ahead, 13 pieces per side
  - **Hard**: AI looks 4 moves ahead, 13 pieces per side
  - **Expert**: AI looks 5 moves ahead, 13 pieces per side
- 🧠 Advanced minimax AI with alpha-beta pruning
- 🎯 Strategic gameplay with mandatory capture rules
- 🎨 Enhanced 3D visual effects with shadows and highlights
- 🔊 Interactive sound effects (toggle on/off)
- ✨ Animated pulse effects for selections and moves
- 📱 Improved touch support for mobile devices
- 🚀 Single HTML file - no installation needed!

**How to Play:**
- **Online:** Visit https://deepakaola.github.io/JOD/
- **Offline:** Open `warp-lines.html` or `index.html` in any modern web browser

**Game Rules:**
- 2 players (Red vs Green)
- Move pieces along the graph edges to adjacent empty nodes (straight lines only)
- Jump over opponent pieces to capture them
- If you can capture, you MUST capture
- Win by eliminating all opponent pieces or blocking their moves

**AI Features:**
- Smart move calculation using minimax algorithm
- Evaluates position based on piece count, mobility, center control, and piece positioning
- Difficulty levels scale from beginner-friendly to expert challenge
- Easy mode starts with fewer pieces for faster games

Red's Turn

Warp Lines - Game Rules

Objective

Eliminate all opponent pieces or block them so they cannot move.

Setup

  • Red starts at the top with 13 pieces
  • Green starts at the bottom with 13 pieces
  • Center position (E2) starts empty

How to Play

Turn Structure:

  • Players alternate turns, Red goes first
  • Click your piece to select it, then click where to move
  • Each turn you must make exactly one move

Normal Move (Step):

  • Move your piece to an adjacent empty node
  • You can only move along the lines you see on the board

Capture Move (Jump):

  • Jump over an enemy piece to an empty space beyond it
  • The jumped piece is removed from the game
  • If you can capture again after landing, you MUST continue jumping
  • You can change direction between jumps

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Mandatory Capture Rule

  • If ANY of your pieces can capture, you MUST make a capture
  • You cannot make a normal step when a capture is available

Winning

  • Win by eliminating all enemy pieces
  • Win if opponent has no legal moves

Game Modes

  • PvP: Two players on same device
  • PvC: Play against computer
Red Wins!
